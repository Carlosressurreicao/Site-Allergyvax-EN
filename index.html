Clinic growth and patient engagement

Reimagining immunotherapy together

AllergyVax helps clinics combine mobile technology, cloud management, and patient communication to support long-term immunotherapy treatment.

  1. Make your patients the center of allergy treatment using AllergyVax mobile technology.
  2. Integrate the mobile app and cloud platform to manage immunotherapy and offer patients a modern service experience.
  3. Use the cloud platform to store immunotherapy treatment data and interact with patients through the app.
  4. Support better adherence to allergen immunotherapy during long treatment journeys.
  5. Strengthen patient engagement and improve the operational value of immunotherapy in your clinic.
  6. Automate routine staff tasks such as follow-up surveys and allergy shot or allergy drop reminders.
  7. Monitor patient adherence using AllergyVax on the computer or in the management app.
  8. Communicate directly with patients by email, SMS, or chat, improving operational efficiency.
  9. Send automated SMS messages reminding patients about allergy shots or allergy drops.
  10. Reduce consultation time by pre-assessing symptoms with ACT and RCAT questionnaires.
  11. Receive periodic treatment outcome data through patient self-assessment of rhinitis and asthma control.
  12. Increase patient satisfaction with mobile technology applied to immunotherapy treatment.
  13. Improve the perception of treatment efficacy by sharing ACT and RCAT results with patients.
  14. Build loyalty to immunotherapy treatment over 3 to 5 years through patient interaction and education.
  15. Raise the perceived quality of your clinic by offering patients personalized mobile technology.
  16. Use AllergyVax to support clinic marketing strategies through SMS, email, and chat.

AllergyVax is a support application for managing allergen immunotherapy (AIT) treatment. This treatment can be highly effective in controlling allergic symptoms, with the possibility of remission of allergic diseases for prolonged periods, reducing or even abolishing the use of medication. The main objective of AllergyVax is to support interaction between doctor and patient, increasing adherence, safety, and treatment effectiveness.
